Meet our Designer

​Zenia Horton has been designing award-winning kitchens, baths, and custom built ins for residential and commercial clients throughout Ontario for the past 20 years. Zenia is a real designer- an artist..which is really just a nice way of saying she's kind of quirky! Working with Zen is entertaining, enlightening and engaging. Her passion and artistry is captured in every design and exceeding your expectations is her mission. Her goal as your designer is to maximize the functionality, beauty and enjoyment of your space; she has a reputation for thinking outside-the-box and consistently delivers best in class solutions and "wow-factor" design.

"Being able to work alongside the skilled craftsmen and installers at K&K to help deliver your vision has been a dream come true."

Our Process

Contact us to find out how you can meet with one of our designers to share your ideas and vision. We will be asking you for rough dimensions of your space ad listening to your ideas, needs, budget and timeline.

Design and Presentation

After we receive all your information we will go hard to work for you to give you the very best use of your space while we incorporate all our years of design knowledge of function, ergonomics, balance, and symmetry. We meet with you to present our concept and ideas and together figure out the very best possible solution to maximize your space to the fullest.
